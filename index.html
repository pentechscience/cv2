Profile

Ms. Shabina Shukoor brings over 20 years of experience as a Project Manager in Information Technology, for infrastructure, enterprise systems, application development and integration projects, for large clients with complex, high security/high availability and critical systems (CN, Desjardins, Motorola, TD Insurance, Scotiabank, Morgan Stanley, others). Experience with University and governmental clients include: Florida Atlantic University (FAU), Polytechnique Montréal, Communications Research Centre, Agence de Mobilité Durable, Ville de Montréal and Canadian Space Agency. Extensive experience in managing external suppliers, contracts, PO`s, procurements and RFP process. As a ‘servant leader’ who volunteers as a career-development coach, Shabina is also PMP certified and has trained over 500+ project managers and technical resources in various business, technical and management skills, including PMP certificate exam preparation (at iCapVantage). As a certified Professional Engineer \ who is always keeping on top of new industry trends, Ms. Shukoor has a demonstrated track-record of very quickly self-learning and mastering the tools, methods and technologies required by her clients, while also training entire IT departments.
